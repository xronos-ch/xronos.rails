## Current XRONOS

XRONOS is currently maintained by Martin Hinz and Joe Roe at the Institute of Archaeological Sciences, University of Bern, supported by the [Swiss National Science Foundation](https://www.snf.ch/en) ([SNSF Project #198152](https://data.snf.ch/grants/grant/198153)).

## Contributing Datasets

XRONOS is continuously updated with data from a number of existing databases, including:

- [14SEA](http://www.14sea.org/) — maintained by Agathe Reingruber and Laurens Thissen
- [ADRAC](https://github.com/dirkseidensticker/aDRAC) — maintained by Dirk Seidensticker, Wannes Hubau and Katharina V. M. Jungnickel
- [AIDA](https://github.com/apalmisano82/AIDA) — maintained by Alessio Palmisano, Andrew Bevan, Alex Kabelindde, Neil Roberts & Stephen Shennan
- [BDA](https://bda.huma-num.fr) — maintained by Thomas Perrin
- [CalPal](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.7215741) — maintained by Bernhard Weninger
- [caribbean-14C](https://github.com/philriris/caribbean-14C) — maintained by Phil Riris
- [CONTEXT](http://context-database.uni-koeln.de/) — maintained by U. Böhner & D. Schyle
- [EUBAR](https://telearchaeology.org/eubar-c14-database/) — maintained by Gacomo Capuzzo
- [IRDD](https://sites.google.com/site/chapplearchaeology/irish-radiocarbon-dendrochronological-dates/download) — maintained by Robert M. Chapple
- [MedAfriCarbon](https://theia.arch.cam.ac.uk/MedAfriCarbon/) — maintained by Giulio Lucarini
- [MesoRAD](https://www.mesorad.com/) — maintained by Julie Hoggarth and Claire Ebert
- [NERD](https://github.com/apalmisano82/NERD) — maintained by Alessio Palmisano, Andrew Bevan, Dan Lawrence and Stephen Shennan
- [p3k14c](https://www.p3k14c.org/) — maintained by the PEOPLE3000 Working Group
- [RADON](https://radon.ufg.uni-kiel.de/) — maintained by Martin Hinz & Christoph Rinne
- [rxpand](https://github.com/jgregoriods/rxpand) — maintained by Jonas Gregorio de Souza
- [Southern African Radiocarbon Database](https://github.com/emmaloftus/Southern-African-Radiocarbon-Database) — maintained by Emma Loftus

## Published Compilations

- [Böhner, U. and Schyle, D., 2004.](https://web.archive.org/web/20161224034255/http://context-database.uni-koeln.de/) Radiocarbon CONTEXT database.
- [Capuzzo, G., Boaretto, E., and Barceló, J. A., 2014.](https://doi.org/10.2458/56.17453) EUBAR: A database of 14C measurements for the European Bronze Age. A Bayesian analysis of 14C-dated archaeological contexts from Northern Italy and Southern France. *Radiocarbon* 56(2): 851–869.
- [Courtney Mustaphi, Colin, 2016.](https://doi.org/10.7910/DVN/NJLNRJ) Radiocarbon dates from eastern Africa in the CARD2.0 format. *Harvard Dataverse*.
- [Crema, E.R., Kobayashi, K., 2020.](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jas.2020.105136) A multi-proxy inference of Jōmon population dynamics using Bayesian phase models, residential data, and summed probability distribution of 14C dates. *Journal of Archaeological Science* 117: 105136.
- [D'Errico, F., Banks, W. E., Vanharen, M., Laroulandie, V., and Langlais, M., 2011.](https://doi.org/10.4207/PA.2011.ART40) PACEA Geo-Referenced Radiocarbon Database. *PaleoAnthropology* 2011: 1–12.
- [Katsianis, M., Bevan, A., Styliaras, G., and Maniatis, Y., 2020.](https://doi.org/10.5334/joad.65) An Aegean history and archaeology written through radiocarbon dates. *Journal of Open Archaeology Data* 8: 5.
- [Manning, K., Colledge, S., Crema, E., Shennan, S. and Timpson, A., 2016.](https://doi.org/10.5334/joad.40) The Cultural Evolution of Neolithic Europe. EUROEVOL Dataset 1: Sites, Phases and Radiocarbon Data. *Journal of Open Archaeology Data* 5: 2.
- [Martínez-Grau, H., Morell-Rovira, B., and Antolín, F., 2021.](https://doi.org/10.5334/joad.72) Radiocarbon Dates Associated to Neolithic Contexts (Ca. 5900–2000 Cal BC) from the Northwestern Mediterranean Arch to the High Rhine Area. *Journal of Open Archaeology Data* 9: 1.
- [Palmisano, A., Bevan, A., and Shennan, S., 2018.](https://doi.org/10.5334/joad.43) Regional Demographic Trends and Settlement Patterns in Central Italy: Archaeological Sites and Radiocarbon Dates. *Journal of Open Archaeology Data* 6: 2.
- [Williams, A. and Ulm, S., 2014.](https://doi.org/10.5284/1027216) AustArch: A Database of 14C and Luminescence Ages from Archaeological Sites in Australia. *Archaeology Data Service*.
